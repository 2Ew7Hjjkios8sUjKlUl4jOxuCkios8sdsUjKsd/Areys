### Planner Response

To remove the blank space for unused infants, you should use the **Dynamic Loop** feature I added to the code. 

Instead of typing `{IFNT1}`, `{IFNT2}`, etc. on separate lines in Word, you should replace all of them with these **three lines**:

---
**`{#infantList}`**
`{name}`
**`{/infantList}`**
---

### Why this works:
1.  **If you have 1 infant**: The app will only write **one line** for that infant.
2.  **If you have 0 infants**: The app will **remove all three lines** completely. No blank space will be left at all!
3.  **If you have 5 infants**: The app will automatically create **5 lines**, one for each name.

### How to do it:
1.  Open your Word template.
2.  Find where you currently have the 5 infant lines.
3.  Delete all 5 lines.
4.  Type the three lines exactly as shown above.
5.  Save and try generating again!

This is the "pro" way to handle lists in Word so that the document stays clean and professional no matter how many infants are traveling.
